Study Drills
This exercise is diﬃcult enough, but if you want some additional challenges, do the following:
1.
Create an alternative interpreter that uses a parser generator like SLY (https://github.com/
dabeaz/sly). Once you have the ABNF this may end up being much easier, but it could be
harder with a language like BASIC. You’ll have to do it and ﬁnd out.
2.
Try to make a version of “structured BASIC” that has functions, loops,
if-statements
, and
everything you’d ﬁnd in an older non-OOP language like C or Pascal. This is a
huge
task, so I
suggest if you attempt it to
not
write an RDP parser by hand. Use a tool like SLY to generate
your parser and save your brain for more important things.
