Replacing Data
I’m going to show you an alternative way to insert data that helps with atomic replacement of rows. You
don’t necessarily need this too often, but it does help if you’re having to replace whole records and don’t
want to do a more complicated
UPDATE
without resorting to transactions.
In this situation, I want to replace my record with another guy but keep the unique id. The problem is, I’d
have to either do a DELETE/INSERT in a transaction to make it atomic, or I’d need to do a full UPDATE.
Another simpler way to do it is to use the
REPLACE

INSERT
